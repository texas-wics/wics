We're a Team & Community of Technologists

Women in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a network and community of women in Computer Science. Our mission is to encourage and support women in computing through outreach, professional development, academic initiatives, and social events.

Start Exploring

What We Do

Mentorship

Industry mentorship, graduate women panels, faculty lunches, and more.

Academics

Course study sessions, study groups, bull sessions, office hours, etc.

Network

Network with our FoCS Sponsors, corporate receptions, and attend on-site tours.

Learn

We put on different makers workshops and host an annual hackathon.

Social

Come to our fun events like pumpkin painting, clothes exchange, annual holiday party, volleyball events, and more.

Outreach

Follow the leader, collborate with girl scouts and ASB.
